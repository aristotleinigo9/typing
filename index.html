Speed and accuracy can only come from training your fingers to know the exact location of every key on the keyboard. FDSA JKL; are called the home keys. Your fingers rest on the home keys. From the home keys, you can reach all the other keys on the keyboard. Use your left hand for FDSA and your right hand for JKL;. The keyboard diagram shows which home key finger is used for each key. When any finger is not actually pressing a key, you should keep it resting lightly on its home key.
